<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-mce-fragment="1" data-pm-slice="1 1 []" dir="auto" id="foo" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1">I just released the next update for the OpenScanCloud: An application, where you can easily select an image folder or zip file to upload for photogrammetry processing. This is as close to a <em data-mce-fragment="1">one-click-solution</em> as possible. And the best part (at least for you): no programming needed ;)</span></p>
<div style="text-align: center;"><img alt="" src="/assets/img/posts/2021-10-08-openscancloud-osc-desktop-uploader-for-windows-beta/2021-10-10-osc-uploader-windows-600x600.jpg" style="float: none;"/></div>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-mce-fragment="1" dir="auto" id="dth8" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1">Just download the executable file from the <a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" data-mce-fragment="1" data-mce-href="https://github.com/OpenScanEu/OpenScanCloud/blob/main/README.md#current-functionality--desktop-uploader-for-windows--download" href="https://github.com/OpenScanEu/OpenScanCloud/blob/main/README.md#current-functionality--desktop-uploader-for-windows--download" target="_blank"><u data-mce-fragment="1">GitHub Project Page</u></a>. You just need to unpack the zip file and start the <em data-mce-fragment="1">OpenScanCloud.exe</em>.</span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-mce-fragment="1" dir="auto" id="54v1q" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1">Insert your personal token, which can be obtained by sending a personal email to <a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" data-mce-fragment="1" data-mce-href="cloud@openscan.eu" href="cloud@openscan.eu" target="_blank"><u data-mce-fragment="1">cloud@openscan.eu</u></a> (which will be automated in the future). After the first start of the application, you need to enter the token, and you are good to go. </span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-mce-fragment="1" dir="auto" id="3t1bn" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1">Select an image set, click <em data-mce-fragment="1">start processing. </em>The uploading and processing is fully automated, and you will shortly get the resulting 3d model by email.</span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-mce-fragment="1" dir="auto" id="foibu" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1">Note that the application is totally free (and hopefully I can keep it free/<a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" data-mce-fragment="1" data-mce-href="https://www.buymeacoffee.com/openscan" href="https://www.buymeacoffee.com/openscan" target="_blank"><u data-mce-fragment="1">donation-based</u></a> in the future). Your data will be transferred through Dropbox and stored/processed on my servers. I will use those image sets and resulting 3d models for further research, but none of your data will be published without your explicit consent!</span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-mce-fragment="1" dir="auto" id="em1p1" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1">Please help me test the application! And feel free to share this post, so that more people can join the OpenScan Project :)</span></p>
