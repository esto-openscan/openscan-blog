<h3 class="vGBkk sWzLp" data-mce-fragment="1" data-pm-slice="1 1 []" dir="auto" id="foo" indentation="0" level="3" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1"><strong data-mce-fragment="1">The Micro</strong></span></h3>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-mce-fragment="1" dir="auto" id="dbbg9" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span data-mce-fragment="1">It has been quite some time since my last visible online activity, and there have been a lot of changes over the last few months. But with change comes opportunity, and here is the first of many new upcoming 3D scans:</span></p>
<div style="text-align: center;"><img alt="" src="/assets/img/posts/2021-08-18-major-update-openscan-micro-future-1/2021-08-13-openscan-micro-600x600.webp" style="float: none;"/></div>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" data-pm-slice="1 1 []" dir="auto" id="9jahi" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>After getting to know this wonderful little new human, my focus on many things shifted. Nevertheless, OpenScan will be a major part of my future and there is still so much more to do and to explore. A major benefit of being a father is that time became much more limited, and thus I need to focus and be more efficient.</span></p>
<h3 class="vGBkk sWzLp" data-pm-slice="1 1 []" dir="auto" id="dl0nn" indentation="0" level="3" textstyle="[object Object]"><span><strong>Consistency</strong></span></h3>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="3m2mc" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>Those following the OpenScan project for a while know that focusing is not my strength and that I get distracted quite easily. Therefore, various promising side-projects and interesting developments ended silently and never got documented or published at all. </span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="ebi3t" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>Another issue of the last years is my very irregular publishing of content. I have never had a plan, when and which content should be posted where. And this led to quite some large gaps on the various channels (e.g. my last noteworthy YouTube video is from April 2020 ^^)</span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="5gg5p" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>Now, I am in a totally new situation, where time is so much more limited. I partially explain my past inconsistency with the fact that I did not need to make a plan. I had plenty of time to spare and not many obligations. But it seems to be the opposite now. Spare time is super rare and obligations skyrocketed. </span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="29t1o" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span><strong>My vision for the future of OpenScan</strong></span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="3oll4" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>So I have spent quite a few nights on what seems to be a plan, that won't just end up in my endless to-do-list-bucket. I have settled on a weekly post-schedule where this blog will be the central point for publications. From there, I will cross-post on the different platforms depending on the content. I will also publish all 3d scans on various sites like <a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" href="https://sketchfab.com/openscan" target="_blank"><u>Sketchfab</u></a>, <a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" href="https://www.thingiverse.com/openscan" target="_blank"><u>Thingiverse </u></a>and similar and try to create more <a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCG3IgwSIFFlc77Luf9VVlYw" target="_blank"><u>YouTube </u></a>content from some of the topics.</span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="31f95" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span><a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/142108429832711" target="_blank"><u>Facebook</u></a> and <a class="Y-s64 XzMaT" data-hook="WebLink" href="https://www.reddit.com/r/OpenScan/" target="_blank"><u>Reddit</u></a> will be the place for detailed discussions and there will be a monthly Newsletter summarizing most posts. So please feel free to join those channels :)</span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="bo4c6" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>For now, there will be a four-week-cycle with four main topics:</span></p>
<ol>
<li>OpenScan Projects &amp; Updates - I will post about the current progress and (side-) projects, next there will be a post about the multi-camera scanner and maybe a tour of my relatively new workshop.</li>
<li>Scan of the Month - Presenting a scanned object with the focus on the occurring challenges.</li>
<li>Tutorials - Explaining certain scanning related topics in more detail. Next up will be a tutorial on object preparation with chalk/scanning spray. I might also review some other existing scanners in the mid-future.</li>
<li>Scan the World - This is a totally new section for me, but I would love to contribute to this wonderful initiative, where people around the world are digitizing their cultural heritage. For now, I will go out and scan some local sculptures, artworks and historical buildings, which are quite abundant in my wonderful town of Halle (Saale).</li>
</ol>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="c5ebr" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>Btw, with this plan, I have already managed to focus and fill most of the topics with content for the upcoming weeks, which makes me really happy.</span></p>
<p class="j1LEL va-er" dir="auto" id="bm1rr" indentation="0" textstyle="[object Object]"><span>I am very open to your suggestions. Which topics are you interested in? What would you like to see being scanned?</span></p>
