<p data-v-a47e4ba7="">For years, OpenScan has focused on affordable 3D scanning for smaller objects.<br data-v-a47e4ba7=""/>Today, we are ready to take the next logical step.</p>
<p data-v-a47e4ba7="">Meet<span>&nbsp;</span><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">OpenScan Halo</strong>: a photogrammetry rig made of multiple synchronized belts around the Earth, designed to deliver<span>&nbsp;</span><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">100% scan coverage, 24/7</strong>.</p>
<ul data-v-a47e4ba7="">
<li data-v-a47e4ba7=""><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">No turntable needed, passive rotation provided by existing planetary motion.</strong></li>
<li data-v-a47e4ba7=""><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">Improved occlusion handling through orbital belt redundancy.</strong></li>
<li data-v-a47e4ba7=""><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">Affordable 3D scanning for the masses. All of them.</strong></li>
<li data-v-a47e4ba7=""><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">Seamless metadata synchronization across all longitudes</strong></li>
<li data-v-a47e4ba7=""><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">Multi-hemisphere capture for improved underside coverage.</strong></li>
<li data-v-a47e4ba7=""><strong data-v-a47e4ba7="">Cloud, fog and haze processing in the OpenScanCloud.</strong></li>
</ul>
<p data-v-a47e4ba7="">Early internal tests have shown promising results for terrain, buildings, coastlines, and other medium-sized planetary features.</p>
<p data-v-a47e4ba7="">We are currently exploring strategic partnerships with rocket-owning billionaires.</p>
<p data-v-a47e4ba7="">More details:<span>&nbsp;</span><a data-v-a47e4ba7="" href="https://halo.openscan.eu/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://halo.openscan.eu/</a></p>
